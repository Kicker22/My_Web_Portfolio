About Me

Hello, I'm Matt Sullivan, a skilled and passionate web developer with 5 years of experience in crafting exceptional digital experiences. I am driven by a genuine love for technology and the ever-evolving landscape of web development. My goal is to create aesthetically pleasing, functional, and user-friendly websites that exceed expectations and help businesses thrive online.

With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I have the ability to bring designs to life and build interactive web applications that engage users. I am proficient in various front-end frameworks such as React, Angular, and Vue.js, and have a deep understanding of responsive design principles to ensure seamless experiences across devices.

In addition to my front-end expertise, I have a solid understanding of back-end development. I am well-versed in server-side languages like Python, PHP, and Ruby, and have experience working with databases such as MySQL and MongoDB.
